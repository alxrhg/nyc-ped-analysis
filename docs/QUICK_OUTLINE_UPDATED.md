## Narrative spine

**The GIS chapter (tract maps + tables + peer NTAs) is the centerpiece.** Lit, framework, methods, qual, and discussion **orbit** those outcomes: they prepare, interpret, and limit what the maps can claim—not equal “parallel studies.”

## One-sentence thesis

**Reproducible tract-level GIS** shows how **Houston–Canal** sits on **walking exposure, pedestrian harm pressure, mixed use, and transit context** relative to Manhattan (and selected peers); read with **NYC history and qual**, that spatial diagnosis supports **discussing** LTN-style options—**not** simulated traffic or before–after proof.
